FAQs

Can I stay on Free as long as I want?

Yes. Free is meant to prove there are real buyers in your niche. Upgrade when you want more volume and automation.

What happens on Yearly?

You’re billed once for 12 months at ~20% off. You keep the discount even if we add new Pro features during your term.

Do you offer onboarding help?

Pro includes quick-start guidance. VIP includes a dedicated success engineer who tunes signals and scoring to your stack.

Security & compliance?

We follow least-privilege access, audit trails, and vendor reviews. VIP supports custom DPA/SLA.
